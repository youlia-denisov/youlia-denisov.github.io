About me

Streamlit App

WattWise — Electricity Dashboard

Multi-user Streamlit dashboard for analysing household electricity consumption from Israeli IEC exports. Runs a full pipeline: preprocessing, K-means clustering, four outlier-detection methods, weather correlation via Open-Meteo, and a discount plan comparison engine. Every browser session is fully isolated — no data leaks between users.

⚠️ Demo mode: Several features require a real IEC export file — upload yours to unlock them.

Interactive Dashboard

NGT Tracker — Gene-edited Agricultural Products

Interactive dashboard tracking 206 gene-edited agricultural products across 60 organisms and 17 countries. Maps the global CRISPR/NGT pipeline from Discovery to Market — covering development stage, company type, trait category, and geography. Rebuilt from Power BI into a fully browser-based app: no licence required, responsive layout, animated bar chart race (2016–2024), and a world map not in the original. 43% of tracked products are already at Approved or Marketing stage.
